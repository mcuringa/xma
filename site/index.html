Educational technology is an interdisciplinary field found at the intersection of learning sciences, media studies, computer science & information systems, and teaching.

Two Tracks

The teaching track certifies existing teachers for a second certification as a NYS Educational Technology Specialist. They prepare to integrate the latest technologies into their current teaching or to move into school-wide or district-wide technology positions.

The design track prepares candidates for careers in educational technology in higher education, corporate or public learning, and educational media/software design and production.
